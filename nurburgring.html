First-timer tips
🐢Lap 1 — go slow. 21 km of blind crests and surprise corners. Learn before you push.
👁Keep eyes up and far ahead — the road changes constantly.
⬅Overtake on the LEFT only. Move right if someone's faster.
🟡Yellow flag = 50 km/h immediately. No overtaking until flag is clear.
💧Any fluid leak — pull over, get behind the armco, call +49 2691 302 9111.
🔒SLK roof must be fully closed and latched at all times on track.
📸Dashcam OK — handheld filming during lapping is not allowed.
⚠️Breidscheid Bridge has a 50 km/h speed limit — marshals enforce this.

• High-visibility reflective vest — one per person in the car

• First aid kit (Verbandkasten)  •  Warning triangle

• Headlight beam deflector stickers

All available from Halfords or Amazon before departure.

⚠️ If something goes wrong on track

1. Pull car to the side of the track immediately.

2. GET BEHIND THE ARMCO BARRIER — Never stand on the track.

3. Warn oncoming cars from behind the barrier.

4. Call: +49 2691 302 9111

5. Give your location — corner names are on the barriers.

6. Do NOT move cars until marshals instruct you.

7. All towing and recovery costs are YOUR responsibility.
